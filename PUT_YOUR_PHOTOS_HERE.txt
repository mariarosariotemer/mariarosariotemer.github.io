Put photos of the artwork here, plus one profile photo.

Expected file names (used by the page by default):
- profile.jpg          → the About section photo
- piece-1.jpg to piece-6.jpg  → the gallery pieces

You can use different names — just make sure the name in index.html
(inside the ARTWORKS list, and the profile photo line) matches exactly,
including capital letters.

Tips:
- JPG or PNG both work.
- Before uploading, resize/compress large photos so the site loads fast.
  A free tool for this: https://squoosh.app (drag the photo in, export,
  upload the result instead of the original).
- Roughly 1500px on the longest side is plenty for a portfolio image.
